Frequently Asked Questions

What type of art do you feature on this platform?
We showcase a diverse range of contemporary and traditional art, including digital illustrations, paintings, photography, sculpture, and mixed media from both emerging and established artists.
Are the artworks available for purchase originals or prints?
Our catalog includes both original artworks and high-quality limited edition prints. Each listing clearly states whether the piece is an original or a print.
How do I commission a custom piece from an artist?
You can request a commission by contacting the artist directly via their profile or by using our custom commission form. Be sure to describe your concept and desired size or medium.
Do you support emerging or independent artists?
Yes! One of our core missions is to support up-and-coming and independent artists by providing them a global platform to showcase and sell their work.
Can I submit my own artwork to be featured?
Absolutely. We welcome artist submissions through our “Join as an Artist” page. All submissions go through a curation process to ensure quality and fit.
What licensing options are available for digital art?
We offer a variety of licensing options including personal, commercial, and editorial use. Each artwork will specify its licensing rights upon download or purchase.
Is the artwork on your site exclusive to Artful Vision?
Some pieces are exclusive while others may be available on other platforms. Listings clearly indicate exclusivity where applicable.
How is the artwork packaged and shipped?
All artwork is carefully packed with protective materials to ensure it arrives safely. Framed or canvas pieces are boxed securely and shipped via trusted couriers.
Do you offer certificates of authenticity with the artwork?
Yes, all original artworks and limited edition prints include a signed certificate of authenticity either from the artist or Artful Vision.
Can I preview how a piece of art will look in my space?
We offer a virtual preview tool on selected listings where you can visualize the artwork on your wall using augmented reality or room mockups.
What if my artwork arrives damaged?
If your order arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photos. We will arrange a replacement, refund, or repair as needed.
How do I know if an artwork is limited edition?
Limited editions are labeled clearly on the artwork page. You will also receive a numbered certificate to confirm its edition and authenticity.
Are your artists from specific regions or global?
We represent a global community of artists, bringing unique cultural perspectives and styles from around the world to our platform.
What makes your curation process unique?
Our team of curators selects artwork based on originality, artistic merit, and alignment with our values—ensuring that every piece tells a compelling story.
Can I gift an artwork to someone else through your site?
Yes! Simply select the gift option at checkout and we’ll include a personalized message and ensure the piece is beautifully packaged for gifting.
